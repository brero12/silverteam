Rules for Memory Game

  1. The scope consists of n different image card types with pairs of cards that have the same image.
  2. Each turn, you flip over two cards (one at a time, so you can see the first one before picking the second).
  3. If the two cards match, then they are keep shown on the screen, otherwise, they are returned to hide.
  4. The game ends when all the cards are keep shown on the screen. in that moment time will stop.
