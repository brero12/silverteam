What would you like to know?

BVR

Stands for "Beyond visual range", this section will cover knowledges about the usage of active radar guided missiles such as AIM-120B/C AMRAAM missiles

WVR

Stands for "Within visual range", this section will covers the usage of infrared guided missiles such as AIM-9 Sidewinders and guns used in close range fights

Dogfights and BVR tactics

This section will demonstrate the tactics in 2 air-to-air fight concepts with 2 weapons mentioned above.
